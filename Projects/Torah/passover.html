Passover

Reflection

Being spared, protected, concealed, shielded. These are the words that come to mind when I think about Passover (Pesach). This is one of those awesome days that make you think about Yahuah’s awesome power and might, and the extent that He is willing to go for His people. Our Father wants us to live in true freedom where there is no oppressive force over us who’s only desire is to destroy us.
